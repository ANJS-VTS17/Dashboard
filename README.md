#  Product Sales Dashboard – Power BI (Blue Theme)

This dashboard visualizes stock quantity and sales performance by:
- Product
- Country & City
- Gender
- Date Range

## 📊 KPIs
- Total Sales
- Top Product
- Stock by Gender
- City-wise and Country-wise Sales
